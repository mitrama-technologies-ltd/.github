### 🛠 Technical Excellence

We adhere to high engineering standards to ensure data integrity and reliability in low-bandwidth environments:

* **Offline-First Architecture**: Systems are designed to work seamlessly without internet, utilizing local databases (Drift/SQLite) and background synchronization services.
* **Data Security & Compliance**: Fully compliant with the **Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023**. We use AES-256 encryption at rest and in transit.
* **Intelligent Sync**: Utilizing a "Pull-then-Push" strategy to resolve data collisions in multi-device environments.
* **Signed Ledger Systems**: Our inventory logic (StockIt) uses signed integers for stock movements to maintain a mathematically perfect audit trail.
